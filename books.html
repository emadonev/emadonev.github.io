My favorite books

Cogheart

by Peter Bunzl

A cogheart adventure

SOME SECRETS CHANGE IN A HEARTBEAT

"Lily's life is in mortal peril.Her father is missing and now silver-eyed men stalk her through the shadows.What could they want from her?

With her friends - Robert, the clockmakers son, and Malkin her mechanimal fox - Lily is plunged into a murky and menacing world.Too soon Lily realizes that those who she hold dear may be the very ones to break her heart..."

My comments:

The book was a mystery, full of danger, adventure and great loss, but nevertheless it is a must read!

Moonlocket

by Peter Bunzl

A cogheart adventure

IT'S HARD TO ESCAPE THE SECRETS FROM THE PAST

"Storm clouds gather over Lily's and Roberts summer when criminal mastermind the Jack of Diamonds appears.For Jack is searching for the mysterious Moonlocket - but that's not the only thing he wants.

Suddenly, dark secrets from Robert's past plunge him into danger: Jack is playing a cruel game that Robert is a oart of.Lily and Malkin, the mechanimal fox, must stay one step ahead before Jack plays his final, deadly card..."

My comments:

Full of daring and adventure, dark secrets and surprisies nobody expects.Also a must read!

Spaced out

"There's nowere to hide on the world's first moon base.After all it's only the size of a soccer field.So when the commander of Moon Base Alpha mysteriously vanishes, the Moonies are a total loss.
Though he may be just twelve years old, Dashiell Gibson is the best detective they've got.But this mind-boggling mystery pushes Dash to his limits.Plus, it turns out he's got something else on his mind - and the fate of hummanity might be at stake."

My comments:

This is a great mystery, funny and weird characters and everything comes in it's place in the end.A very mysterious read!

The wild robot

by Peter Brown

"When robot Roz opens her eyes for the first time, she discovers that she is alone on a remote, wild island. She has no idea how she got there ot what her purpose is - but she knows she needs to survive. But as Roz slowly befriends the animals, the island starts to feel like home - until one day, her mysterious past comes back to haunt her."

My comments:

A very emotional book, about how different kinds should take care of each other and trust each other, rather than fight and hate. A must read!
